We approved an 8-point standard to keep grounding and isolation consistent across the whole vehicle.

## Scope
- Chassis-body grounding points and wiring harness isolation rules.
- Minimum clearance and shielding requirements separating high/low voltage lines.
- Every sub-team reviews its electronic designs against this standard.

From now on, all electrical/electronic designs will be reviewed against this document.
